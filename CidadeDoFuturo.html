Cidade do Futuro

About the Project

“Cidade do Futuro” is an interactive projection art experience developed for the Alfândega do Porto exhibition, exploring future cities through immersive motion graphics and ambient sound.

My Role

I led the creative direction, animation, and integration of multimedia elements using After Effects and TouchDesigner. The focus was on narrative motion and reactive visuals that respond to audience flow.
