My Story 21 years

I'm Pedro Costa, a Multimedia student at ESMAD - School of Media Arts and Design (Politécnico do Porto). I specialize in motion design, video editing, and VFX, with professional proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and Unity.

My journey in multimedia began early - since I was 14, I've been working with video editing as both a freelancer and content creator. Over the years, I've expanded my skillset to include 3D modeling, game development, photography, and creating immersive spaces using tools like MadMapper and TouchDesigner.

Beyond technical skills, I'm deeply committed to the multimedia community. I founded and served as president of NEME (Núcleo de Estudantes de Multimédia da ESMAD) and have been actively involved in the students' association of ESMAD, most recently as coordinator of the communication department.

I'm always looking for new challenges and interesting projects where I can apply my diverse skill set. If you think we could work together or just want to talk about multimedia, feel free to reach out.
